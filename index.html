Tej Deep Pala

I am currently a 1st year PhD student at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), advised by Prof. Soujanya Poria. My research focuses on Large Language Model Reasoning, and Reinforcement Learning.

Previously, I earned my Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science and Design from Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD) in May 2024 with Highest Distinction.

If you are interested in collaborating or chatting, feel free to reach out to me at ptejdeep@gmail.com.
